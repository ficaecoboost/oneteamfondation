Our first fundraiser will involve our founder walking more than 1000 km. The walk will begin in Belgrade, Serbia and end in Athens, Greece.

We have long worked with Goran Krivokuca and Tennis Park, Belgrade and we will continue to do so as we support their values and particular training style. The aim of the walk is to raise funds in the shape of donations that will initially be allocated to our first project which will enable 10 children to have three months of free tennis lessons. The remaining funds will be allocated to music, art and workshops for children.
