FOUNDER
Our Founder

Without tehnology we are powerless!!!

I was born in Dubrovnik to Serb parents from Hercegovina. At 7, after a year in Dubrovnik, we moved to the UK. Despite moving, I always loved Yugoslavia and felt destined to return. In 2007, I moved to Serbia and from 2009 to 2014, I directed and owned Tenis Klub Trim in Belgrade. Running the club, I met many top players, both WTA and ATP. A friend once asked me to arrange a meeting with Novak Djokovic. It was challenging, but I managed. Novak's mental strength and desire to give back to children inspire me. I share a similar passion for helping children. I believe Serbia can improve through future generations. By giving children love and support, primarily through sports, they can grow into better individuals. This will inspire them to help others and strive for positive change.
